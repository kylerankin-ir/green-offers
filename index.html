What we cover

Four ways to a greener, cheaper home.

Solar panels

Generate your own electricity from daylight and lean less on the grid — with any surplus earning you money back.

Battery storage

Store the energy you generate (or buy cheaply overnight) and use it when you need it, day or night.

Heat pumps

A cleaner, efficient way to heat your home that can replace an ageing boiler — and you may qualify for government grants.

EV charging

Charge your electric car at home, overnight and on cheaper rates — faster and more convenient than public points.

About this Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Policy outlines how we use and share the information you provide. How your data is used

With your consent, we collect your details when you request a quote and may contact you by phone, SMS, email or web chat to confirm your requirements. With your consent, your data may be shared with our marketing and installer partners so they can provide the products or services you've asked about. Prior to sending third-party marketing communications we seek your explicit consent, and you can withdraw it at any time.

What we store and for how long

We typically store your name, address, email, phone number and details of the product or service you're interested in. We retain personal data only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes it was collected, including legal, regulatory and accounting obligations.

Your rights

Under data protection law you have the right to access, correct, erase, restrict or object to the processing of your personal data, to data portability, and to withdraw consent. You can read more at the ICO. To exercise any right, contact us using the details above.

Who we share your data with

We are a marketing and lead-generation company and will share your details with the installer and marketing partners named at the point of collection, so they can contact you about the products or services you requested. We require our partners to respect the security of your data and to process it in line with the law and our instructions. We only share your data with your consent or as you would reasonably expect, and you can withdraw consent at any time.

How to opt out

Complaints
